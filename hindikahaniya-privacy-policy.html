Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to the mobile application “Hindi Kahaniya(हिंदी कहानियाँ)”, developed and maintained by RGS Technologies.

1. Information Collection and Use

Hindi Kahaniya(हिंदी कहानियाँ) does not collect, store, process, or transmit any personal or sensitive user data.

We do not collect information such as name, email address, phone number, location data, device identifiers, contacts, or any other personally identifiable information.

2. App Content and Purpose

Hindi Kahaniya(हिंदी कहानियाँ) is a storytelling application that provides a wide range of Hindi stories across multiple categories.

Users can read and listen to stories without creating an account or providing any personal information.

3. Audio Record Permission (Microphone Access)

This app requests Audio Record (Microphone) permission solely to enable voice command features.

The microphone is used only to allow users to:

  • Navigate to the next or previous story using voice commands
  • Control the app using voice (for example, closing the app)

No audio is recorded, stored, saved, or transmitted to any server or third party. All voice processing happens locally on the user’s device.

4. Data Sharing

We do not share any user data with third parties. Since no personal data is collected, there is nothing to share.

5. Third-Party Services

This app does not use any third-party services that collect, track, or analyze user data.

6. Data Security

As Hindi Kahaniya(हिंदी कहानियाँ) does not collect or store any personal data, there is no risk of data misuse, leakage, or unauthorized access.

7. Children’s Privacy

Hindi Kahaniya(हिंदी कहानियाँ) is suitable for users of all age groups. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13 years of age.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
