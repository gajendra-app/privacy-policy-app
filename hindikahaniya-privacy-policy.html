<h2>1. Information Collection and Use</h2>

<p>

Hindi Kahaniya(हिंदी कहानियाँ) does <strong>not collect</strong>, store, process, or transmit

any personal or sensitive user data.

</p>

<p>

We do not collect information such as name, email address, phone number,

</p>


<h2>2. App Content and Purpose</h2>

<p>

Hindi Kahaniya(हिंदी कहानियाँ) is a storytelling application that provides

a wide range of Hindi stories across multiple categories.

</p>

<p>

Users can read and listen to stories without creating an account

or providing any personal information.

</p>


<h2>3. Audio Record Permission (Microphone Access)</h2>

<p>

This app requests <strong>Audio Record (Microphone)</strong> permission solely to enable

<strong>voice command features</strong>.

</p>

<p>

The microphone is used only to allow users to:

</p>

<ul>

    <li>Navigate to the next or previous story using voice commands</li>

    <li>Control the app using voice (for example, closing the app)</li>

</ul>

<p>

<strong>No audio is recorded, stored, saved, or transmitted</strong> to any server or third party.

All voice processing happens locally on the user’s device.

</p>


<h2>4. Data Sharing</h2>

<p>

We do <strong>not share</strong> any user data with third parties.

Since no personal data is collected, there is nothing to share.
